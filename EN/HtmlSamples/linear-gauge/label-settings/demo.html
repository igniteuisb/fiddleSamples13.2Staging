Fever Temperature

F°
You have a fever when your temperature rises above its normal range. What's normal for you may be a little higher or lower than the average normal temperature of 98.6 F (37 C). High fevers between 103 F (39.4 C) and 106 F (41.1 C) may cause: Hallucinations; Confusion; Irritability; Convulsions; Dehydration.

Beachgoers along the Jersey Shore have had to make a choice this summer: wither in the sun or shiver in the water. That's because steady southwest winds are pushing warm water away from the beach and pulling colder water up from the bottom of the ocean, experts say. The process is called upwelling and water temperatures are nearly 15 degrees below normal for this time of year in some parts of the state. Thursday (06/12/2012) afternoon, Atlantic City Steel Pier thermometers were near 55 degrees. The average temperature for this time of year is 69 degrees.
